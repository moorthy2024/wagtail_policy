About this Manual

To deliver its services as a National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) Registered Provider, Wagtail Services must comply with the NDIS Quality and Safeguards Framework. Wagtail Services must:

  • Comply with the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) Act 2013 (Cth)
  • Comply with the NDIS Terms of Business for Registered Providers and any Conditions of NDIS Registration
  • Obtain and maintain accreditation against the NDIS Practice Standards

The NDIS Practice Standards are quality standards that govern how Registered NDIS providers must deliver services. The Standards that relate to Wagtail Services are:

Schedule 1 – Core Module

  1. Rights of Participants and responsibilities of providers
  2. Provider governance and operational management
  3. Provision of supports
  4. Support provision environment

This Policy and Procedure Manual sets out the policies and procedures that govern Wagtail Services’ operations and service delivery. All policies and procedures comply with the NDIS Practice Standards as well as relevant Commonwealth and State Government legislation and regulations applicable to Wagtail Services’ service delivery. The manual is intended to complement all State and Commonwealth legislation and does not override any Acts of Parliament or other legal requirements.

Wagtail Services staff must comply with the policies and procedures in this manual. Failure to follow these policies and procedures will be treated seriously and may result in disciplinary action. Staff must refer to the manual on a regular basis and keep up-to-date with any changes.

The policies and procedures within this manual will be reviewed regularly by the Leadership Team, with review timeframes based on a risk management approach.

PLEASE NOTE:

  • For simplicity, where the term ‘staff’ is used throughout this Manual, it refers to the paid employees, volunteers, contractors and any other person undertaking work for or on behalf of Wagtail Services.
  • Where the term ‘Participant’ is used throughout this Manual, it refers to the NDIS Participant who is a client of Wagtail Services, and who may be supported by parents/guardians or other supporters who are making decisions on the NDIS Participant’s behalf.
  • Where the term ‘Manager’ is used throughout this Manual, it refers to the Directors when they act within a managerial capacity, and/or the staff member employed as Manager.
